WOF - WARRANT OF FITNESS

A warrant of fitness (WoF) is a regular check to ensure that your vehicle meets required safety standards. Our certified inspectors thoroughly check all safety aspects including brakes, tyres, lights, steering, and structural integrity. We provide quick and efficient service to get you back on the road safely.

  • Certified Inspectors
  • Quick Turnaround
  • All Vehicle Types
  • Safety Report

Did you know?

Your vehicle must have a current WoF to be legally driven on the road. Driving without a valid WoF can result in fines and insurance issues. We recommend checking your WoF expiry date regularly.
